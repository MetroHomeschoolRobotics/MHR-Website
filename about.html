About Us

MHR is based out of Kansas City, Missouri. Our Goal is to reach the surrounding community of private, public, and homeschooled high school students (Age: 14-18 | Grade: 9th-12th) to teach the skills needed to become leaders.

Since 2006 we have been providing an environment designed to teach real-world engineering, problem solving and teamwork by competing in FIRST Robotics and hosting offseason learning workshops.

The FIRST Robotics season starts in January and goes through March. During that time we design and build a robot to complete the game that is provided to us in January at kickoff.
