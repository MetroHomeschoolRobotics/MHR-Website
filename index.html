    Welcome to MHR we are based out of Kansas City, Missouri. Our Goal is to reach the surrounding community of private, public, and homeschooled high school students (Age: 14-18 | Grade: 9th-12th) to teach the skills needed to become leaders.

    Since 2006 we have been providing an environment designed to teach real-world engineering, problem solving and teamwork by competing in FIRST Robotics and hosting offseason learning workshops.


    Science

    Scientific method approach to prototyping, Biomechanics research to learn how nature solves problems.

    Technology

    Programming, Electronics, Networking and Data Analysis.


    Engineering

    Brainstorming, Design, Stress Analysis, Manufacturing, Assembly and Project Management.

    Mathematics

    Algebra, Geometry, and Calculus


    Teamwork
