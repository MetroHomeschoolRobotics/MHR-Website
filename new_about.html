About Us

Welcome to MHR we are based out of Kansas City, Missouri. Our Goal is to reach the surrounding community of private, public, and homeschooled high school students (Age: 14-18 | Grade: 9th-12th) to teach the skills needed to become leaders.

Since 2006 we have been providing an environment designed to teach real-world engineering, problem solving and teamwork by competing in FIRST Robotics and hosting off season learning workshops.
